# Customer Churn Analytics with Statistical Validation

## Problem Statement
Customer churn leads to revenue loss and higher acquisition costs.
This project analyzes churn drivers using EDA and statistical testing.

## Tools Used
• Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
• Statistical Testing (Chi-Square, T-Test)
• Jupyter Notebook

## Key Insights
• Month-to-month contracts show highest churn
• Early-tenure customers are most at risk
• High monthly charges correlate with churn
• Auto-payment reduces churn probability

## Business Impact
The analysis provides actionable strategies that can reduce churn
and improve customer lifetime value.

## Recommendations
• Convert customers to long-term contracts
• Improve onboarding experience
• Offer loyalty-based pricing
• Promote auto-pay adoption
